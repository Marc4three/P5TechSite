📊 Project Status Calculation

Project status is determined by comparing actual progress against expected progress based on task due dates.

🎯 Status Categories

On Track: Meeting or exceeding expected progress
At Risk: 5-15% behind expected progress
Off Track: More than 15% behind expected progress

📈 Progress Calculation

With Due Dates:
  • Expected progress is calculated based on elapsed time between earliest and latest due dates
  • Actual progress is based on completed tasks
  • Days behind is calculated from the difference between expected and actual progress
Without Due Dates:
  • Status defaults to "On Track"
  • No days behind calculation
  • Progress is based solely on completed tasks
