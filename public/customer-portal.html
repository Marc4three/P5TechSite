How Forecast Status Works

Project progress is calculated based on:

  • Start date: First task moved to To-Do
  • Expected progress: Based on elapsed time vs. total duration

Task weights by status:

  • To-Do: 0%
  • In Progress: 50%
  • QA/Testing: 75%
  • Done: 100%

Status thresholds:

  • ✅ On Track: Meeting or ahead of schedule
  • ⚠️ At Risk: 5-15% behind schedule
  • ❌ Off Track: More than 15% behind
